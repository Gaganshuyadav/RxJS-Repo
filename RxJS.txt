Conditions for RxJS

=================== RxJS Observable Behavior ===================

1. After complete():
   - No further next(), error(), or complete() calls will occur.
   - Observable finalizes and automatically unsubscribes.

2. After error():
   - Observable finalizes and automatically unsubscribes.
   - No further next() or complete() calls will occur.

3. After unsubscribe():
   - No further emissions (next, error, complete) will happen.
   - Teardown/cleanup logic (if defined) will be executed.

4. Manual Unsubscribe:
   - Can stop a subscription anytime via subscription.unsubscribe().

5. Auto-Unsubscribe on Error:
   - Emits error and automatically unsubscribes unless catchError is used.

6. Auto-Unsubscribe on Complete:
   - Emits complete and automatically unsubscribes.

7. Conditional Unsubscribe:
   - Automatically unsubscribes using: take, takeUntil, takeWhile, first.

8. Cleanup on Unsubscribe/Error/Complete:
   - Teardown logic will run on unsubscribe, error, or complete.

9. No Emission After Complete:
   - Any next() or error() after complete() will be ignored.

10. No Emission After Error:
    - Any next() or complete() after error() will be ignored.

11. Emission Filtering:
    - Use filter, debounceTime, distinctUntilChanged, etc. to block emissions.
